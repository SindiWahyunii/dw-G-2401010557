CHOOSE & ENJOY

Whether you crave classic flavors or daring combinations, this is where your culinary journey begins. Indulge your cravings and savor every bite as you create your personalized pizza experience with Soletta Pizza.

Pepperoni Pizza

Soft dough inside, covered in rich fresh tomato sauce, creamy melted mozzarella, and savory, slightly spicy pepperoni. When baked it produces crispy edges full of distinctive aroma.

American Pizza

Pizza with thick, chewy dough, covered in rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and optional toppings such as pepperoni, sausage, and mushrooms. Bold flavors in every bite!

Margherita Pizza

Classic pizza with a thin, crispy crust, topped with fresh tomato sauce that’s sweet and tangy, soft mozzarella that melts perfectly, and fresh basil leaves that add a signature aroma.

Hawaiian Pizza

A tropical delight in every bite! Sweet, juicy pineapple pairs perfectly with savory smoked ham and melted mozzarella cheese, all brought together by our signature tomato sauce and a sprinkle of fragrant oregano.

Cheese Lovers Pizza

Layers of melted mozzarella, rich cheddar, and savory parmesan create the perfect balance of creamy and crunchy textures in every bite. The special cream sauce adds a creamy touch and pairs perfectly with this premium cheese.

Veggie Pizza

A burst of freshness with crisp bell peppers, savory mushrooms, juicy tomatoes, and tangy black olives, topped with melted mozzarella and herb-infused tomato sauce. A perfectly balanced, delicious veggie delight!
